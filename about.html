Innovate. Communicate. Circulate.

Elemental TV is the UK’s first and only dedicated webTV channel for healthcare technology - a platform for thought-leadership and trail-blazing that brings the public and private sectors together to share health tech’s themes, challenges and triumphs through the use of technology.

Elemental TV is part of the Elemental brand which has a rich history, spanning 25 years in the healthcare technology sector, and as part of this brand, we naturally feel passionate about fostering the sharing of ideas and innovation to support the improvement of services.

Our programmes are driven by the innovation that healthcare providers need and want to share, and are recorded in our central London studios in front of a live audience.

We make this content available, not only through the Elemental TV platform, but also via social media, via our NHS contributors’ websites and through our sponsors’ channels as well.
