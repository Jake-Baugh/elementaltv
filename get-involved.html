Get Involved

If you’re a trust with an innovative project to share, or a tech provider with a recent strategic implementation, then we’d like to hear from you. Elemental TV is a webTV channel dedicated to health technology that showcases innovative projects through engaging video content. Elemental TV is accessible to view online, free of charge.

Follow us on Twitter and LinkedIn, email ben@weareelemental.uk or call 0808 168 6426 to start planning your 15 minutes of fame!

Partner with us

Innovation in healthcare is driven by the professional bodies that guide it, and we’d like to hear from you too. If you represent a professional body, consultancy or media organisation, and would like to propose content for a programme, join a panel, our studio audience,or perhaps host a show, get in touch.
